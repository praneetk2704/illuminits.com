English

The English Literature Club is one of the finest and the most elite clubs that the ILLUMINITS proudly holds. Under the aegis of the club heads, the English Literature Club has long been living up to the legacy of maintaining the standard of English language in the campus be it spoken or in writing. The club also organizes various competitions round the year thriving to uplift the confidence of the students and promoting global standards of the language.

Chinmoy Shekhar Das

Bangla

The Bengali Club of Illumints: Literary, Publication, and Fine Arts Society, NIT Silchar has come up with a solemn vow to keep up the age of Bengali culture and binding. We aim towards promoting admiration for the Bangla Language among the NIT family and try to show respect towards this by organising various events. One such worth mentioning event is the "Rabindranath Tagore Memorial Day"

Debakshi Dey


Vocal Skills

Vocal Skills Club aims at development of Oratory skills among its members thorugh regular events such as debates, extempore, declamation, group discussion etc. The club is one of the most prominent at Illuminits and has brought many laurels to it in various fields.

Pranshu Kumar Gautam


NITS MUN

NITSMUN or NITS Model United Nations is one of the newest ventures undertaken by ILLUMINITS and is seen over by the NITSMUN Secretariat. NITSMUN is organised as a part of Incandescence.
