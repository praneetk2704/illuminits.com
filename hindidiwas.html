Hindi Diwas

Illuminits: Literary, Publications and Fine Arts Society celebrates Hindi Diwas annually with great enthusiasm. This year, the event was named “Sanchipt Aakhyaan”. It served to promote and propagate the Hindi language. Its importance was demonstrated by events and competitions held like Kavi Sammelan, Poem recitation, Ghazal and Music. Many prominent personalites were invited as chief guest and prizes worth 10k were given. The event also served as a patriotic reminder to people of their common roots and unity.
